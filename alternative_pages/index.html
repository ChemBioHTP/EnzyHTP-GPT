EnzyHTP Web Application

A Virtual Assistant for Enzyme Molecular Modeling.

Enlarge the active site of an enzyme to expand their substrate scope.
Sample conformations for a lasso-peptide to determine their structures using NMR.
Does mutation X lead to drug resistance of SARS-CoV-2?
Does surface mutation X change the shape of the binding site Y through dynamics?

The repository of EnzyHTP is available at https://github.com/ChemBioHTP/EnzyHTP

Our website will be officially launched in August 2024. Stay tuned!
